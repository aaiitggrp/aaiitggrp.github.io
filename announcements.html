Regarding Internship: Guideline for Applicant

Students willing to do summer internship with me should either apply through the department call (generally opens in Jan-Feb) or through summer research fellowship program of Indian Academy of sciences. Any direct request coming to me without adopting one of the above mentioned approaches will not be considered. I do not consider any application for winter internship.

One of the internship positions is available for a student who has prior experience in developing R packages for CRAN .
